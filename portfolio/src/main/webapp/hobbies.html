Hobbies

Sports

Tennis

Tennis is easily defined as my favorite sport. I started playing with my father when I was around four years old. He would take my brother and I out to play even though we were not able to properly hit the ball over the net yet. Over the years, what used to be an activity meant to bring us together as a family transitioned into an activity I am deeply passionate about.

I joined my first team and got my first coach as a freshman in high school. I joined varsity right away and played at that level for all four years. Throughout my high school career I earned many league awards and finished first or second in the league for three consecutive years.

I still love tennis to this day, and I will continue practicing to participate in local tournaments.

Soccer

Before I fell in love with tennis, I enjoyed soccer. Prior to entering high school, I had no friends that played tennis. So, I directed my energy towards soccer.

I played for many years, with a brief period of inactivity due to health concerns, but I was never that good. I gradually started to enjoy soccer less and less, but I will always watch the FIFA World Cup. Basketball

It wasn't until my junior year of high school that I actually started to enjoy basketball. I wish I had shown interest in it earlier, but it wasn't until I saw friends of mine on the basketball team play that I was like, "WOW."

Basketball is my favorite sport to watch and my favorite player is Luka Doncic. Even though Luka Doncic is my favorite player and I was raised in New York all my life, my favorite team is neither the Dallas Mavericks or a New York team (disappointing, I know). My favorite team is the Houston Rockets because James Harden made me a fan of the sport. The way he plays is just too fun to watch. Even though he still doesn't have a championship ring, I know he will get one before he retires.

Given all that I have said, my prediction for this year's champion is the Los Angeles Lakers. If the break due to quarantine hadn't occurred I believe the Los Angeles Clippers were going to win, but now that Lebron James is well rested I know he will carry his team to another ring.

Instruments

I would love to learn as many instruments as possible. A dream of mine for when I have my own place is to be able to have an entire room filled with instruments, and at any given time be able to pick up an instrument and play it like a boss. I don't know what it is, but instruments are just so fascinating to me.

The first musical instrument I learned to play was the violin. I started playing in fourth grade and continued playing until I graduated high school. I went to two competitions with my high school orchestra and won first place both times. While I was playing I didn't think it would be something that I missed (mostly because I did not like my music teacher), but after not playing in years I do miss it a lot. Missing the violin, however, led me to pick up the guitar. I am now currently learning it and am still very new to the guitar, but I hope to get better and better.

I also learned the piano when I was younger, but I stopped playing for a few years and I have now forgotten everything I had learned. I don't own a piano anymore, but I have a MIDI Keyboard that I connect to my computer and mess around with in FL Studio from time to time. FL Studio, for those unaware, is a music production software and I use it to make songs. Although I like to make my own songs, I have not released anything to the public. I make music for myself, but maybe one of these days I will share what I have.
